3.Preceding Question Prompting

Example 1: “Before explaining the concept of Stock Market, ask three questions that help understand the topic better.”

Answer:

1.What do you already know about buying and selling shares?
2.Have you heard of companies raising money through public investment?
3.Why do you think people invest in stocks or shares?

 Explanation:
 Buying and selling shares means purchasing small ownership portions of a company, which can increase or decrease in value over time. Companies raise money from the public through stock markets by offering shares, allowing investors to fund their growth. People invest in stocks mainly to earn profits through price appreciation and dividends, as well as to build long-term wealth.

Example 2: “Before describing the benefits of Education, ask two questions related to students’ learning experiences.” 
Answer:

1.How has learning new subjects helped you improve your skills or knowledge?
2.What challenges have you faced in your education, and how did you overcome them?

Explanation:
Learning new subjects has helped me improve my knowledge, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. I have faced challenges like understanding difficult concepts and managing time, but I overcame them through regular practice, asking for help, and staying consistent in my studies.
